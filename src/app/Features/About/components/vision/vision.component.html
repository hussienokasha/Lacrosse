{{ "Vision" | translate }}

We aspire to provide an opportunity to enjoy a different sport and achieve global excellence, becoming one of the best lacrosse communities.

{{ "Mission" | translate }}

The Saudi Lacrosse Federation provides national leadership, infrastructure, and resources to support the growth of the sport, enrich participant experiences, and enhance and develop lacrosse in all its forms.

{{ "Goals" | translate }}

To contribute to achieving the goal of sports participation, expand the federation's grassroots base, and build infrastructure and organization to enhance the practice of the game.
